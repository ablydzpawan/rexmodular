REXMODULAR

Established in 2014

RexModular is an award winning house builder based in Leeds. RexModular was established in 2014 and our team has over 30 years’ experience in construction and residential developments. RexModular has quickly gained a reputation for building high quality homes.

RexModular is built with care by our dedicated team of professionals, we hand select the best materials to provide an unrivalled specification, combined with building in the finest locations across Yorkshire, let your next house be a RexModular.
